Chat Commands (Viewer-Usable)
=============================

Prefix: !

Economy
- !bal [username]
  Shows coin balance for you or the optional username.

- !fish
  Fish once per cooldown to earn coins.

- !slots <bet>
  Slot machine bet.

- !roulette <bet> <red|black|even|odd|0-36>
  Roulette bet.

Quotes
- !quote
  Shows a random quote.

- !quote <number>
  Shows a specific quote number.

- !quote add <text>
  Saves a new quote.

Song Requests
- !sr <song/link>
  Adds a song request.

- !sr list
  Shows current queue preview.

Shop
- !shop
  Shows shop items.

- !shop buy <item>
  Buys an item.

- !shop inv
  Shows your inventory.
